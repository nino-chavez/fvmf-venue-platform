## Introduction

Welcome to The Venue Aurora blog team! This guide will help you create and publish engaging blog content using Sanity Studio, our content management system.

**What is Sanity Studio?**

- No coding knowledge required

**What You Can Do:**

- Add images and videos

- Optimize posts for search engines

## Creating Your First Blog Post

### Step 1: Start a New Post

1. Click **"Blog Posts"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"+ Create"** button in the top-right
3. Select **"Post"** from the dropdown

### Step 2: Fill Out the Basics

**Required Fields:**

1. **Title** (required)
   - Keep it compelling and under 60 characters
   - Example: "Behind the Scenes: Chicago Blues Legend Willie Buck"

2. **Slug** (auto-generated)
   - This creates the URL for your post
   - Example: `behind-the-scenes-chicago-blues-legend-willie-buck`
   - Click "Generate" if it doesn't auto-populate

3. **Author** (required)
   - Click the dropdown and select your name
   - If you don't see your name, ask an admin to create your Author profile

4. **Published At** (required)
   - Click the calendar icon
   - Select today's date for immediate publishing
   - Or select a future date to schedule the post

**Optional But Recommended:**

5. **Excerpt**
   - A 1-2 sentence summary (150-160 characters max)
   - This appears in search results and blog listings
   - Example: "Go behind the scenes with blues legend Willie Buck as he prepares for his return to The Venue Aurora stage."

6. **Main Image**
   - Click "Upload" to add a featured image
   - **Best practices:**
     - Use high-quality images (at least 1200px wide)
     - Action shots, artist photos, or venue moments work best
     - File size under 2MB
   - Add **Alt Text** for accessibility (describe what's in the image)
   - Add a **Caption** if needed (optional)

7. **Categories**
   - Click "Add item" to select 1-3 relevant categories
   - Examples: "Artist Spotlight", "Event Recap", "Community"

### Step 3: Write Your Content

The **Body** section is where you write your article using the rich text editor.

**Text Formatting:**

- **Headings**: Use H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections

- **Bold**: Select text â†’ Click **B** button

- **Italic**: Select text â†’ Click *I* button

- **Links**:
  1. Select the text you want to link
  2. Click the link icon (ðŸ”—)
  3. Paste the URL
  4. Click "Insert"

- **Lists**:
  - Click the bullet list icon for unordered lists
  - Click the numbered list icon for ordered lists

**Adding Images:**

1. Click the **"+"** button at the start of any line
2. Select **"Image"**
3. Upload or select an existing image
4. Add Alt Text (required for accessibility)
5. Add Caption (optional)

**Adding YouTube Videos:**

1. Click the **"+"** button at the start of any line
2. Select **"YouTube"**
3. Paste the full YouTube URL (e.g., `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xxxxx`)
4. The video will embed automatically on the live site

**Blockquotes:**

For pull quotes or artist statements:
1. Click the paragraph dropdown
2. Select "Quote"
3. Type your quote

Example:
> "The Venue Aurora has one of the best listening rooms in the Midwest. The acoustics are incredible!" - Willie Buck

### Step 4: SEO Settings (Optional but Recommended)

Scroll to the **SEO** section to optimize for search engines:

1. **Meta Title**
   - Leave blank to use the post title, or customize
   - Keep under 60 characters

2. **Meta Description**
   - Write a compelling 150-160 character summary
   - This appears in Google search results

3. **Keywords**
   - Add 3-5 relevant keywords (press Enter after each)
   - Examples: `blues concert aurora`, `willie buck`, `live music illinois`

4. **Open Graph Title/Description**
   - How your post appears when shared on Facebook, LinkedIn
   - Usually you can leave these blank (uses Meta Title/Description)

### Step 5: Featured Post Toggle

Toggle **"Featured"** ON if this should appear in the "Featured Posts" section on the blog homepage. Only the 3 most recent featured posts will display.

## Blog Post Best Practices

### Content Guidelines

**Length:**
- Aim for 500-1,500 words
- Artist spotlights: 800-1,200 words
- Event recaps: 400-800 words
- Guides/tutorials: 1,000-1,500 words

**Tone & Voice:**
- Conversational but professional
- Passionate about music
- Welcoming to newcomers
- Authentic and genuine (no corporate jargon)

**Structure:**
1. **Opening Hook** - Start with an interesting fact, question, or story
2. **Context** - Provide background information
3. **Main Content** - Deliver on the promise of your headline
4. **Conclusion** - End with a call-to-action or forward-looking statement

**Call-to-Action Examples:**
- "Get tickets to [Artist Name]'s next show â†’"
- "Sign up for our newsletter to hear about upcoming events"
- "Follow us on Instagram @VenueAurora for more behind-the-scenes content"

### Image Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use high-resolution photos (minimum 1200px wide)
- Get permission for artist photos
- Credit photographers in captions when applicable
- Use venue photos from official gallery
- Compress large files before uploading

**Don't:**
- Use blurry or low-quality images
- Post photos without permission
- Forget alt text (hurts accessibility and SEO)

### Writing Headlines

**Good Headlines:**
- "5 Things You Didn't Know About Blues Legend Willie Buck"
- "Behind the Scenes: Preparing The Venue for Sold-Out Shows"
- "Meet the Volunteers Who Make The Venue Aurora Special"

**Weak Headlines:**
- "Concert Review" (too vague)
- "New Blog Post" (not descriptive)
- "Check This Out" (no information)

**Tips:**
- Use numbers when relevant ("5 Tips...", "3 Reasons...")
- Include the artist or event name
- Promise value ("How to...", "Guide to...", "Behind the Scenes...")
- Keep under 60 characters when possible

## SEO Optimization

### Why SEO Matters

Good SEO helps people find your posts through Google, Bing, and other search engines. This brings more readers and potential audience members to The Venue.

### Quick SEO Checklist

**Before Publishing:**
- [ ] Compelling title under 60 characters
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 characters)
- [ ] 3-5 relevant keywords
- [ ] Main image with alt text
- [ ] At least one H2 heading in content
- [ ] 1-3 categories selected
- [ ] Internal links to other blog posts or venue pages
- [ ] External links to artist websites/social media (opens in new tab)

### Internal Linking

Link to other blog posts and venue pages:
```
Check out our [Artist Spotlight series](/blog/category/artist-spotlight) for more stories.
```

### External Linking

Always link to:
- Artist websites and social media
- Ticket purchase pages (Eventbrite)
- Related press coverage

**How to make links open in new tab:**
The system handles this automatically for external links!

## Publishing Workflow

### Before Publishing

1. **Proofread**
   - Check for typos and grammar
   - Read aloud to catch awkward phrasing
   - Use a tool like Grammarly if needed

2. **Preview** (if available)
   - Some Sanity setups support preview mode
   - Ask your admin if preview is enabled

3. **Check All Required Fields**
   - Title
   - Slug
   - Author
   - Published At date
   - Body content

### Publishing Options

**Publish Immediately:**
1. Set "Published At" to today's date/time
2. Click **"Publish"** button in top-right
3. Confirm you want to publish
4. Your post goes live instantly!

**Schedule for Later:**
1. Set "Published At" to a future date/time
2. Click **"Publish"**
3. The post won't appear on the website until that date/time
4. Great for planning content in advance

### After Publishing

**View Your Post:**
1. Go to `https://venueaurora.com/blog`
2. Your post should appear in the list
3. Click to read the full article

**Share on Social Media:**
1. Copy the post URL (e.g., `https://venueaurora.com/blog/your-post-slug`)
2. Share on:
   - The Venue Aurora Facebook page
   - Instagram (link in bio or Stories)
   - Twitter
   - Email newsletter

**Monitor Engagement:**
- Check for comments (if enabled)
- Respond to reader questions
- Share reader feedback with the team

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem: "Publish" button is disabled**
- **Solution**: Check that all required fields are filled:
  - Title
  - Slug
  - Author
  - Published At

**Problem: Images won't upload**
- **Solution**:
  - Check file size (must be under 10MB)
  - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
  - Try compressing the image first

**Problem: Can't find my Author profile**
- **Solution**: Ask an admin to create one for you

**Problem: Post isn't showing on website**
- **Solution**:
  - Check the "Published At" date (might be in the future)
  - Make sure you clicked "Publish" (not just "Save")
  - Wait 2-3 minutes for cache to clear

**Problem: YouTube video won't embed**
- **Solution**:
  - Use the full YouTube URL (not shortened youtu.be links)
  - Make sure the video is public (not private/unlisted)
  - Format: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`

**Problem: Accidentally deleted a draft**
- **Solution**:
  - Check the document history (clock icon)
  - Sanity saves version history
  - You can restore previous versions
