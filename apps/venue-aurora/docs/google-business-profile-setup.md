# Google Business Profile Setup Guide for The Venue Aurora

## Why Google Business Profile is Critical

Google Business Profile (GBP) is the **#1 local SEO ranking factor** for music venues. It powers:
- Google Maps results
- Local Pack (top 3 results)
- Knowledge Panel on search
- Mobile "near me" searches
- Voice assistant location queries

**Impact:** Venues with optimized GBP get 5-7x more visibility in local search.

## Verification Methods

Google will ask you to verify ownership:

**Available Methods:**
1. **Postcard** (Most common)
   - Mailed to business address
   - 5-14 days arrival
   - Enter code from postcard

2. **Phone** (May be available)
   - Automated call with code
   - Instant verification

3. **Email** (If available)
   - Verification email
   - Click to verify

4. **Video Verification** (New businesses)
   - Record video tour
   - Show exterior, interior, business operations

**Choose:** Phone or Postcard verification
