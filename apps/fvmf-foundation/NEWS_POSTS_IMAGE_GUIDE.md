# News Posts - Image Upload Guide

**Created:** December 27, 2025

This guide lists all 6 news posts created in Sanity with their corresponding image URLs from the existing FVMF website. Use this to manually add images through the Sanity Studio interface.

---

## How to Add Images

1. Open Sanity Studio: `http://localhost:3001/studio`
2. Navigate to "News & Updates"
3. Open each draft post
4. Download the images from the URLs below
5. Upload to the **Card Image** and **Hero Image** fields
6. Fill in the alt text as provided
7. Publish the post

## Quick Steps for Each Post

1. Visit Sanity Studio at http://localhost:3001/studio
2. Click "News & Updates" in the sidebar
3. You'll see 6 draft posts - open the first one
4. Right-click the Card Image URL above and "Save Image As..." to download
5. In Sanity Studio, click the Card Image field and upload the downloaded image
6. Add the alt text as specified above
7. Repeat for Hero Image (you can use the same image file)
8. Click "Publish" in the top right
9. Move to the next post

---

## Notes

- All images are in PNG or JPG format from the existing FVMF website
- The URLs are from Wix's CDN (static.wixstatic.com)
- Card images are optimized for 3:2 aspect ratio display
- Hero images work well with the 16:9 display on post detail pages
- Most posts can use the same image for both Card and Hero fields
- Post 6 (Dunham Foundation Grant) is marked as "featured" and will appear in the featured section

---

## After Publishing

Once all 6 posts are published with images:
1. Visit http://localhost:3001/news-updates to see the listing page
2. Click on each post to verify the hero image displays correctly
3. Check that the featured post appears in the featured section
4. Verify all images are properly cropped and centered

---

## Troubleshooting

**If images won't download:**
- Copy the URL and paste directly into your browser
- Right-click the image and "Save Image As..."

**If images look wrong in Sanity:**
- Use the hotspot tool to adjust the focal point
- Re-crop if needed to ensure important subjects are centered

**If you want higher quality images:**
- Modify the URL by changing `w_880` to `w_1920` for width
- Change `h_616` to `h_1080` for height
- This will download higher resolution versions
